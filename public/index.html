About this tool

What is this?

CFB Recruiting Pipeline is an interactive map showing college football recruiting — who each program has signed, who they're targeting, and who they lost to competitors. Switch to Player mode to browse uncommitted prospects by state and see their full offer landscape.

School mode

Search or click any FBS program. Solid arcs show committed recruits. Dashed arcs show current pipeline targets (offered, uncommitted). Faint arcs show recruits who were offered but chose another school.

Player mode

Tap any state to see uncommitted recruits from that area. Tap a player to see every school that has offered them, with their committed school (if any) highlighted in gold.

The data

Historical commit data (2015–2025) comes from the College Football Data API. Current pipeline data (2026+) adds offer information from 247Sports. Data updates daily for commits and weekly for offer lists.
